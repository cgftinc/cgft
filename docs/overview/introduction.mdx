# Introduction

cgft is a rl finetuning platform that lets you finetune llms for specific workloads (rag, etc.) without having to be a ml alchemist or an infra neckbeard.

you bring your data and we'll auto-generate rl environments, build reward signals and eval harnesses, and run the training loop end-to-end. when you do want to go deeper, everything is configurable.

cgft handles:
- distributed training + infra scaling (rollouts, environments)
- algorithmic stability (preventing reward hacking, collapse, mode drift, weird regressions)
- experiment tracking (query the model during training, view model predictions, filter by reward)

## why rl finetuning?

Supervised finetuning (SFT) trains models to imitate examples. RL finetuning trains them to **achieve outcomes**.

This lets you directly optimize for what actually matters:
- answer quality and correctness
- retrieval quality (better search + citations)
- tool use and agent behavior
- format, style, and policy constraints
